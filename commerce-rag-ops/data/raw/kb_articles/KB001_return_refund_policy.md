# Return and Refund Policy

Customers can request a return within 30 days of delivery for damaged, defective, or incorrect items. The support team should verify the order, product SKU, delivery date, item condition, and proof such as photos. Refunds should not be promised until eligibility is confirmed. If the item arrived leaking, broken, or unusable, the support team may offer replacement or refund after validation.

High-risk refund answers must cite this policy and should ask for missing order details when the order, SKU, or delivery date is not available.
